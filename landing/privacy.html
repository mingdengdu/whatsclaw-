Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

WhatsClaw ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WhatsClaw Shopify app and website at whatsclaw.xyz. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you install and use WhatsClaw, we collect:

  • Shopify store data: Store URL, access tokens, product listings, order information, and customer data necessary to provide our service.
  • WhatsApp message data: Inbound and outbound messages processed through our AI system for the purpose of automating customer service.
  • Account information: Email address, store name, and billing information.
  • Usage data: Feature usage, conversation logs, and performance metrics to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the WhatsClaw service
  • To process WhatsApp messages and generate AI responses on your behalf
  • To sync your Shopify orders, products, and customer data for automated replies
  • To send service notifications, billing updates, and product announcements
  • To improve our AI models and service quality

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Shopify: Via official Shopify API to read your store data
  • Stripe: For payment processing (they handle your billing data securely)
  • AI providers: Message content may be processed by AI models to generate responses

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon cancellation, store data is deleted within 30 days. WhatsApp message logs are retained for 90 days for support purposes.

5. Customer Data (GDPR / CCPA)

WhatsClaw processes your customers' WhatsApp messages on your behalf. As a Shopify merchant, you are the data controller. We act as a data processor. You are responsible for having appropriate legal basis (e.g., consent) for processing your customers' messages.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data in transit. API tokens and secrets are encrypted at rest. We never store WhatsApp session credentials in plaintext.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@whatsclaw.xyz.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for session management and analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you via email and in-app notification before significant changes take effect.

10. Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@whatsclaw.xyz
